The film is a sequel to Guardians of the Galaxy , Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 2 , Avengers: Endgame and The Guardians of the Galaxy Holiday Special . It is the thirty-second film in the Marvel Cinematic Universe and the second installment of Phase Five . The film was released on May 5, 2023. The film is written and directed by James Gunn and stars Chris Pratt as Peter Quill/Star-Lord , Zoe Saldana as Gamora , Dave Bautista as Drax the Destroyer , Karen Gillan as Nebula , Pom Klementieff as Mantis , featuring Vin Diesel as Groot , Bradley Cooper as Rocket Raccoon , Sean Gunn as Kraglin Obfonteri , Chukwudi Iwuji as High Evolutionary , Will Poulter as Adam Warlock , Elizabeth Debicki as Ayesha , Maria Bakalova as Cosmo and Sylvester Stallone as Stakar Ogord . Production [ ] On March 15, 2017, James Gunn stated that there were plans for a third Guardians of the Galaxy film.

On April 17, 2017, James Gunn announced that he would return to write and direct Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 3 , saying that the film would "conclude the story of this iteration of the Guardians of the Galaxy, and help catapult both old and new Marvel characters into the next ten years and beyond."

He also felt that the three Guardians films would "work together as a whole", telling one story.

On April 27, 2018, Chris Pratt confirmed that he would be reprising his role as Peter Quill/Star-Lord in the film.

On May 29, 2018, Dave Bautista confirmed that he would be reprising his role as Drax the Destroyer in the film.

On June 25, 2018, Kevin Feige stated that James Gunn had completed the first draft of the screenplay and that the film would begin production in early 2019.

On July 20, 2018, Walt Disney Studios severed ties with James Gunn after old tweets from Gunn surrounding topics such as rape and pedophilia resurfaced.

On July 30, 2018, in response to James Gunn's firing, many of the Guardians cast members including Chris Pratt, Zoe Saldana , Dave Bautista, Bradley Cooper , Vin Diesel , Sean Gunn , Pom Klementieff , Michael Rooker , and Karen Gillan issued a statement in support of Gunn.
